How this works
Two pages share the same data: index.html (your clients) and admin.html (this editor). Edits live in your browser instantly — to make them visible to everyone, hit Export data.js and upload the downloaded file to js/data.js on your server. Photos are referenced by URL — paste links from Unsplash, your Drive (use the direct image URL), Cloudinary, or relative paths like images/haldi/01.jpg if you've uploaded files alongside the site.
